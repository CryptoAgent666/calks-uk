### Calks.uk Competitive Advantages

1. **Scale:** 331 calculators vs competitors' 5-30 — broadest coverage in the UK market
2. **Speed:** Static HTML (sub-1s load) vs competitors' server-rendered pages
3. **Privacy:** Client-side calculations (unique selling point for YMYL trust)
4. **Breadth:** 20 categories covering tax, health, construction, farming, immigration, gardening — competitors focus on 1-3 categories
5. **Freshness:** 2025/26 rates with visible "Last Updated" dates

### Calks.uk Weaknesses

1. **Domain age:** Brand new — no backlink profile or domain authority
2. **No blog/editorial content:** Competitors win on informational queries
3. **No comparison/review content:** Missing "best savings accounts" type content
4. **Brand recognition:** Zero — competitors are established names
5. **No Google Analytics / Search Console data yet:** Blind to actual performance

### AI Search Targets
- **Google AI Overviews:** Optimise calculator pages to appear in AI-generated answers for "how much tax on [X]" queries
- **ChatGPT web search:** Ensure llms.txt is crawled; optimise for citability
- **Perplexity:** Structured data + clear answers = high citation potential
- **Bing Copilot:** Bing Webmaster Tools submission; IndexNow for instant indexing

### Key GEO Actions
1. Add more "answer-first" formatting to How It Works sections
2. Create "definition boxes" at top of key calculator pages (e.g., "What is Stamp Duty? Stamp Duty Land Tax is...")
3. Add structured comparison tables where applicable
4. Ensure every worked example has clear, extractable data points
5. Monitor AI citation across platforms monthly
